Trailblazer
(September 2011 - July 2012)

Overview

Trailblazer is a fast-paced action-puzzle game for PC (Windows). You player as an anthropomorphic blob of oil. As the player moves around the grid-based levels, they leave behind a trail of oil - and along with it, fire! The goal of the game is to reach to end of each level before the fire catches up to you, all while avoiding dangers such as traps and enemies.

This project was the very first game that I made (overall and at VGDC UCI).

Status: Complete (Download)

Role

My primary role for this project was level designer.

Levels

  • I co-wrote the level design document with the other designer
    • I rated the difficulty of levels as well as document level statistics (length, fastest completion time, etc)
    • Notes included in this document reflected designer rationale
  • I created levels (a bit differently from the other designer) with our level editor
    • Most of my levels were difficult in general, due to a focus on precise movements and timing (through trial and error)
