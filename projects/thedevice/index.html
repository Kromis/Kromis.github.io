The Device
(May 2013 - June 2014)

Overview

The Device is a wave-based strategy defense game for PC and Android. The goal of the game is to protect "The Device", a floating cube that generates experience orbs, from enemy forces. Gather experience points to level up and unlock new abilities to defeat increasingly difficult waves. Be careful as enemies can also pick up experience and "level up" to become more powerful creatures!

This game started out as a 72-hour VGDC-UCI game jam game and continued development as a VGDC-UCI game project. The game also placed 1st in the 2013 IEEE GameSig Showcase

Status: On Hold

Role

My role for this project was designer. I worked with two other designers on the game mechanics, UI, and levels.

Game Mechanics

  • I collaborated with the designers creating different enemy types
    • This involved tweaking and balancing enemy statistics (speed, health, damage, etc) and coming up with different characteristics for each evolved form (monster abilities and behavior)
    • In addition to balancing enemies, I contributed to designing and balancing items
  • I also worked on game progression, tuning the difficulty and pacing of the games and levels

UI

  • I worked with a designer and programmer on determining the UI and layout for the in-game store and on-screen elements during gameplay

Levels

  • I designed a variety of enemy formations to be used in our level editor and created the levels.
