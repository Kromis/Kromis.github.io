Slicense to Dye
(October 2013 - May 2014)

Overview

Slicense to Dye is a casual, action-puzzle game for Android. Use your knowledge of color (theory) to slice up colored lice and stop them from eating your scalp!

Colored lice appear from the top of the screen and make their way down towards your head. Armed with a color palette, the player must get rid of these lice by slicing them with a matching color. A level ends when (1) there are no more waves of lice or (2) when enough lice reach the your head, leaving you bald. Defeat the boss at the end of each world to conquer it!

Status: Complete (Bug fixes in progress)

Role

My primary role for this project was level designer. I worked with two other designers on game mechanics and levels

Game Mechanics

  • I helped design additional Equipments and Abilities for the lice

Levels

  • I wrote up several design documents detailing level guidelines, level progression, instructions for the level editor, and a general design document
    • Level guidelines were general do's and don'ts for other designers to follow when creating levels
    • Level progression included constructing a roadmap for worlds 0 and 1 for pacing (when to introduce new mechanics and patterns)
    • The level editor was still a work-in-progress so I wrote up instructions on how to use it for other designers
  • I created levels, mainly for "Schoolhouse Lice" and "Leechifer's Feast"
    • Taking note of the level progression roadmap, I would sketch out lice formations and player finger motions for their solutions.
    • I also conducted playtests of the game to gather feedback on the difficulty of each level and iterate on them
