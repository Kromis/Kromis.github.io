Trailblazer

Production:
September 2011 - June 2012

Video
Download

The Game

Trailblazer is a tile-based puzzle-action game developed by students at UC Irvine. The goal of the game is to navigate to the end of a level while solving puzzles and avoiding enemies and traps, especially fire. The player leaves a trail of oil as they move and conveniently enough, a fire will always be chasing you!

The Device

Production:
September 2012 - Present

Video

The Game

The Device is a wave-based defense/strategy game developed by students at UC Irvine as part of a game jam. The goal of the game is to protect an object, "The Device", from waves of enemies. Level up by collecting experience orbs that come from the Device and defeating enemies. But beware, enemies can also level up and evolve into something stronger if they pick up enough orbs!

Slicense to Dye

Production:
September 2013 - June 2014

Video
Download on Android

The Game

Slicense to Dye is a puzzle action game based on color theory. Combine multiple color swipes to take down lice of the corresponding color(s)! Wipe out as many as you can for killer combo bonuses and power-ups! Enemy equipment and powers ensure that your swiping won't be as easy as red, blue, and yellow.
